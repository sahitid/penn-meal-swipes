How It Works

Three simple steps to connect with fellow M&T students over a meal

1

Sign Up

Underclassmen offer meal swipes, upperclassmen request meals. Takes just 2 minutes.

2

Get Matched

Browse profiles and connect with your perfect dining partner based on schedule and preferences.

3

Eat Together

Coordinate schedules and enjoy a meal together at your favorite Penn dining hall.

For Underclassmen

Offer Meal Swipes

Share your extra meal swipes and make new friends in the M&T program! Connect with upperclassmen who want to be part of the dining hall experience.

What happens next?

After submitting, your profile will appear in the browse section where upperclassmen can request to dine with you. You'll be notified when someone wants to connect!

For Upperclassmen

Request a Meal

Browse available meal swipes and connect with M&T underclassmen! Relive the dining hall experience and build connections across class years.

What happens next?

After submitting a request, you'll be matched based on schedule compatibility and preferences. We'll notify you once a match is found!

How to connect

Browse profiles below and click "Request to Connect" to reach out. You can also view the full spreadsheet for detailed availability and contact information.
